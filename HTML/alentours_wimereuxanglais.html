Activites around Casa Mia

During your stays in Wimereux in our gite,
you will be able to enjoy a lot of activites in this very city,
close to your home which is Casa Mia.
Indeed, being located next to the sea, you will be able with your relatives,
to enjoy the calm of the sea, walking in the sand or moreover hiking in the sand dunes...
You will also be able to taste the culinary specialities of our region in the many restaurants close to the sea, and to the gite.

Around Casa Mia you will be able to enjoy the city of Wimereux from the morning to the evening
Come visit and learn the history of our city,
Come and enjoy from de shops that she offers you.
Go for a walk to the dike while eating an ice cream
Or go and visit the port of Wimereux! You will be able to taste or buy there!

After visited Wimereux, you can always go to the cities next to Casa Mia.
Like Calais (located at 30kms), a city of war, known for its dragon that wanders the streets and finishes his journey on the beach...
Or still Boulogne-sur-mer (7kms away), where you can visit the aquatic animals of Nausicaa!

You will be able to enjoy a lot of activities when you come to Casa Mia !
Don't miss this chance to discover a region as beautiful as ours !
See you soon at Casa Mia!
